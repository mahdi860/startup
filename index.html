Mugs Coffees Start up Plan by Mahdi Abdullah Fattah

I have completed my project by making a report on a start-up plan o a coffee house called Mugs Coffee. A startup is a young company founded by one or more entrepreneurs in order to develop a unique product or service and bring it to market. By its nature, the typical startup tends to be a shoestring operation, with initial funding from the founders or their investors.

In this report I will be discussing about the start-up plan for the coffee house. I will be discussing the five following reports: 1.Total Start-up expenses 2.Total Assets & Funding Required 3.Total Liabilities 4.Total Planned Investment 5. Total Capital & Liabilities

Writing a business startup plan is an opportunity to carefully think through every step of starting a company so one can prepare for success. A well-written startup plan can help convince investors or lenders to finance your business.
